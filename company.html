About Microsoft

Microsoft Corporation is a global leader in software, cloud computing, artificial intelligence, and hardware. Founded in 1975, it has become one of the world’s most influential tech companies. Under CEO Satya Nadella, Microsoft delivers innovations like Windows, Azure, Microsoft 365, and GitHub Copilot, while promoting sustainability, digital inclusion, and responsible AI.
